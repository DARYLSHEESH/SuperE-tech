QUARTER TWO

Lesson 1: Platforms

This lesson dives into the various digital platforms shaping today's tech landscape, highlighting how these platforms help people connect, collaborate, and share ideas. It covers everything from social media and websites to collaborative tools, all of which play a vital role in communication and content creation in our digital age.

Lesson 2: Basic Webpage Creation

In this lesson, students will develop the core skills required to design a simple webpage. They will explore HTML (HyperText Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), and basic design techniques to craft websites that are both user-friendly and visually attractive.

Lesson 3: Collaborative ICT Development

This lesson focuses on the importance of teamwork in creating Information and Communication Technology (ICT) projects. It shows how working together through digital tools can streamline the development process, enabling people to share ideas and collaborate in real-time, which boosts creativity and problem-solving.

Lesson 4: Multimedia and ICT

In this lesson, students discover how multimedia elements such as text, images, video, and audio come together with ICT to create compelling content. It covers the role of multimedia in fields like education, entertainment, and communication, and explains how it enriches the user experience in digital media.

Lesson 5: The Power of Social Media

This lesson explores the influence of social media in todayâ€™s world, examining how platforms shape communication, social connections, and the spread of information. It also looks at how social media is used for personal branding, marketing, and activism, highlighting its impact on both individuals and society.

Lesson 6: Social Change

In this lesson, students explore how ICT plays a key role in driving social change. They learn how technology can be harnessed to tackle social issues, raise awareness, and inspire action for positive societal impact. The lesson highlights the power of using technology to make a difference for the greater good.

Lesson 7: Social Advocacy

This lesson explores the concept of social advocacy, highlighting how ICT tools can empower movements and campaigns that promote social justice, environmental sustainability, and human rights. Students learn how digital platforms can be leveraged to raise awareness, rally support, and influence public attitudes towards important causes.

Lesson 8: Sustaining ICT Projects for Social Change

This lesson focuses on how ICT projects designed for social change can be sustained over time. It covers key factors like long-term planning, securing funding, engaging the community, and ensuring scalability to help these projects maintain their positive impact and continue to grow.

Lesson 9: Reflecting on the ICT Learning Process

This lesson encourages students to reflect on their journey through learning ICT. It provides an opportunity to assess their progress, recognize key skills and knowledge gained, and identify areas for further improvement. Through this reflective process, students gain a deeper understanding of how their ICT skills can be applied in real-world contexts.
