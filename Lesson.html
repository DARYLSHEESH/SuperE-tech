Empowerment Technology refers to the use of technology to enhance individuals' ability to make decisions,
gain access to resources, and take control over their lives, both personally and professionally.
It focuses on providing tools, information, and support to individuals or groups that can help them become
more self-sufficient, knowledgeable, and confident in navigating the digital world.
